Frequently Asked Questions

How does the car selling process work?

Start by completing our secure form for a $5.00 fee. After submission, you'll receive an appraisal from our partner, Edmunds, who will analyze your vehicle's details and provide you with most competitive, redeemable cash offer valid for seven days, ensuring a swift and competitive selling experience.

Why is there a $5.00 fee for the form?

This nominal fee ensures the reliability and security of our platform, allowing us to maintain high-quality services and connect you with reputable partners for the best offers.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide basic details about your vehicle, including the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN), make, model, and year. Accurate information helps our partners make the most competitive offers. Please double-check your input before submitting the form.

How do I find my vehicle's VIN?

The VIN is typically located on the dashboard, visible through the windshield on the driver's side, or on the driver's side door frame. It's also found on important documents like your vehicle's registration or insurance papers.

What happens after I submit the form?

Upon form submission, you'll receive an appraisal from our partner, Edmunds, where your vehicle's information will be analyzed. They will present you with a redeemable cash offer valid for seven days, allowing you time to consider the offer.

Can I set my desired selling price?

Our partners evaluate the market and present the highest offers. You have the flexibility to choose the offer that best meets your expectations. For further assistance, please contact us via live chat.

What types of vehicles can I sell through your service?

Our partners accept all types of vehicles based in the USA, including new, used, and damaged cars. You can sell your vehicle quickly and effortlessly, regardless of its condition.

Why is it beneficial to have a Vehicle History Report?
