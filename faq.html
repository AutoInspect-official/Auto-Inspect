Frequently Asked Questions

What is Auto Inspect and what services do we provide?

Auto Inspect offers a comprehensive range of vehicle services, including car verification, detailed history reports, maintenance bookings, and reliable car selling.

Why is it ideal to verify your car history before buying?

Verifying your car's history before purchasing helps uncover potential issues, such as previous accidents, title problems, or odometer fraud, ensuring a safe investment.

What is a VIN and why is it important?

A Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) is a unique code assigned to every vehicle, providing essential details about its history and specifications. It is also known as the chassis number.

Where can I find my vehicle’s VIN?

Your VIN can usually be found on the dashboard, visible through the windshield, or on the driver’s side door frame. It's also present on documents like the car's registration or insurance.

How can I get an accurate vehicle history report?

Auto Inspect offers accurate and free basic info on cars as well as a premium, thorough vehicle history report based on your VIN, covering key details like past accidents, ownership records, recalls, and more.

How can I maintain my car remotely using Auto Inspect?

Our partners provide ideal maintenance services. You can schedule repairs and other services online, ensuring your vehicle stays in top condition without needing to visit a service center.

Can I sell my car on Auto Inspect at my selected price?

Yes, you can sell your car on Auto Inspect. While we evaluate each car based on its condition, please get in touch using our Live Chat where you can finalize your price and enter it when making an offer.

What types of cars can I sell on Auto Inspect?

Auto Inspect accepts all types of cars, regardless of their condition, whether they are new, old, or have multiple issues.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a range of payment options, including credit cards, bank transfers, and secure online payment methods, making it convenient for all customers.

How do I contact Auto Inspect customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through our Contact Us page via email, phone, or our 24/7 online chat for any inquiries or assistance you may need.

We provide reliable car verification and maintenance as well as easy selling services to keep your vehicle in top shape. Trust us for expert advice, detailed inspections, and high-quality service.
