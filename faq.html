Frequently Asked Questions

What is Auto Inspect, and what services do we offer?

Auto Inspect is your trusted partner for vehicle services, providing car verification, detailed history reports, online maintenance bookings, and seamless car selling solutions.

Why is verifying a car’s history essential before buying?

Checking a vehicle's history safeguards you against hidden issues, such as accident records, title discrepancies, or odometer fraud, ensuring you make a confident and informed purchase.

What is a VIN, and why does it matter?

The Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) is a unique identifier for your car, containing critical details about its history, specifications, and past ownership. It is often referred to as the chassis number.

Where can I locate my vehicle's VIN?

You can find the VIN on the dashboard, visible through the windshield, on the driver’s side door frame, or in key documents like the registration or insurance papers.

How do I access a detailed vehicle history report?

Auto Inspect provides accurate and free basic car information. For a more detailed analysis, get our premium vehicle history report using your VIN, covering accidents, ownership records, recalls, and more.

Can I schedule car maintenance online with Auto Inspect?

Yes, our trusted partners enable you to book car repairs and routine checkups online. Keep your vehicle in top shape without the hassle of visiting a physical service center.

Can I sell my car at my desired price on Auto Inspect?

Absolutely. Auto Inspect evaluates your car’s condition and assists in pricing it. Use our Live Chat feature to discuss and finalize your offer.

What types of vehicles can I sell through Auto Inspect?

Auto Inspect accepts all kinds of vehicles—whether new, used, or damaged. Sell your car regardless of its condition quickly and effortlessly.

What payment methods are supported?

We support a variety of secure payment options, including credit cards, bank transfers, and trusted online payment methods, ensuring convenience for all users.

How do I get in touch with Auto Inspect customer support?

Our customer support is available 24/7 through email, phone, or online chat. Visit our Contact Us page for more details.
